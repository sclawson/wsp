Client: Spring Lake Elementary School

Project: Website Redesign

Designer: Shawn Lawson

Document: Project Analysis

Site intent

The website redesign objective of Spring Lake Elementary is to reorganize, condense the current 22 pages into 6 pages of more clear and focused content. This is also to help guide the user more efficiently around the site.

By minimizing the number of pages, we will focus more on the site being more informational, clean and less cluttered. Having the site more organized will help the targeted audience complete the goals of visiting the site often, keeping up with new information at the school and get more involved by accessing the volunteer email request form.

Competitive Analysis

There are many other Elementary schools in the surrounding areas of Spring Lake Elementary. When I looked it up on Alexa, I could only find the actual county schools web statistics. I am finding that with the theme of an elementary school, there's not necessarily a lot of competition, seeing as how the students are placed in schools demographically more than any other factor. So with that, I think it is important to see how the other local schools in the area may be drawing more or less traffic to their site and possibly look into the volume of volunteering.

Targeted Audience

The targeted audience is definitely broad within the school system. We have single moms and dads, blended families and in some cases we have grandmas and grandpas running the roost. By this information, it is important that we target a wide spectrum of audiences.
